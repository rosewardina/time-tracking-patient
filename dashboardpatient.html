Welcome to Unity Health Hospital Emergency Department

At Unity Health Hospital, our Emergency Department is dedicated to providing world-class medical care to patients in critical need, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Our team of highly trained doctors, nurses, and medical professionals is committed to delivering fast, compassionate, and lifesaving treatment in a state-of-the-art facility.

We prioritize patient safety, efficiency, and communication to ensure that every individual receives the immediate care they deserve. With our real-time Patient Priority Tracking System, we maintain constant awareness of the most urgent cases, enabling our staff to make informed, timely decisions for each patient's care.

At Unity Health, we believe in treating every patient with respect, dignity, and a commitment to excellence.
